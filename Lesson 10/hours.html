Availability at a glance

  • 24/7 Emergency & Regular Bookings: We accept bookings any day, any time.
  • Typical response time: Within 2 hours for standard inquiries; under 30 minutes for urgent calls/texts.

How to book

  1. Check availability: Email or call us with your preferred date/time.
  2. Reserve with a deposit: Secure your slot with a deposit (details below).
  3. Confirm details: We'll confirm location, schedule, and any props or special shots.
  4. Shoot day: Our team arrives on time and delivers a professional experience.
  5. Delivery: Edited images/videos delivered within the agreed timeframe.

Pricing & deposits

We ask for a small deposit to secure bookings for prime-time slots or weekend events. Deposits are applied to the final invoice. Custom quotes available for large or multi-day shoots.

Location & travel

Our studio is based locally and we also travel to your preferred locations. For on-location shoots outside a 25-mile radius, travel fees and a small accommodation allowance may apply for overnight jobs.

Yes — we offer full night coverage and can provide lighting and crew as needed.

Cancellation & rescheduling

You may reschedule up to 72 hours before the shoot without penalty. Deposits are refundable if the shoot is cancelled 7 days prior; within 7 days deposits are non-refundable but may be credited toward a future booking.

Client tips for a smooth shoot

  • Plan outfits and bring backups.
  • Arrive 10–15 minutes early to allow time for setup.
  • Share inspiration images ahead of time to help us match your vision.
