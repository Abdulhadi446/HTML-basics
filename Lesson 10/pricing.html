Package Overview

Basic Package — $150

Ideal for portraits, headshots, and short events.

  • 1 hour shoot (studio or nearby location)
  • 10 edited digital images
  • Online gallery delivery

Standard Package — $450

Great for medium events and longer portrait sessions.

  • 3 hour coverage
  • 40 edited images
  • Basic retouching and color correction
  • Online gallery + download

Premium Package — $1200

Designed for weddings, multi-location shoots, and full event coverage.

  • 8 hour coverage
  • All edited images from the day (approx. 200–500)
  • Second shooter included (for weddings)
  • Custom album or highlight video add-on available

Add-ons & common line items

  • Extra hour: $100/hour
  • Second shooter: $200/day
  • Highlight video (2–4 minutes): $400
  • Express delivery (48–72 hours): +30% of package price
  • Travel fees: calculated for jobs outside a 25-mile radius

Deposits, payment & discounts

To secure a booking we require a 25% deposit of the package price. The remaining balance is due on or before the shoot day.

Payment methods: Credit/debit card, bank transfer, cash, or PayPal.

Discounts: Weekday bookings (Mon–Thu) may qualify for a 10% discount on selected packages. Ask about seasonal promotions and repeat-client discounts.

Cancellation & refund policy

Deposits are refundable if a booking is cancelled more than 7 days prior to the shoot. Cancellations within 7 days are non-refundable but deposits may be credited toward a rescheduled date within 6 months.

Frequently asked questions

Can I get just the raw files?
We deliver fully edited images by default. Raw files can be requested for an additional fee.
What if my event runs long?
Extra hours are billed at $100/hour — we will discuss and agree on overtime if needed on the day.
Do you offer refunds if we’re unhappy?
If the delivered work doesn't match the agreed brief, contact us and we'll work to resolve it — re-edits, partial refunds, or reshoots may be offered depending on the case.
