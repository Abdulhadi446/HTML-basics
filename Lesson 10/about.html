Who we are

NM Studio is a premier photography and videography service provider, dedicated to capturing your most cherished moments. With a team of experienced professionals and state-of-the-art equipment, we ensure stunning results for every project.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality visual content that exceeds our clients' expectations. Whether it's a wedding, corporate event, or personal portrait session, we approach each project with creativity and attention to detail.

What we do

At NM Studio, we offer a wide range of photography and videography services to cater to your needs. Our expertise includes:

  • Wedding Photography & Videography
  • Event Coverage
  • Portrait Sessions
  • Product Shoots
  • Editing & Retouching

Where are we located?

Our main studio is located in Pakistan, Karachi.

When It started

NM Studio was founded in 1950 with a vision to provide exceptional photography and videography services. Since then, we have grown into a trusted name in the industry, known for our creativity and professionalism.
